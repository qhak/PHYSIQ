What CALLOUT can and can't judge.

It grades what the photo gives it. No secret dataset flex, no fake lab coat, no pretending a single upload knows your whole training history.

01 · Development

Visible muscle development

CALLOUT looks for visible size, shape, separation, and how much trained tissue is actually readable in the image. If a muscle is hidden, it should not get a confident score.

02 · Shape

Proportion and symmetry

It compares what is visible across the frame: upper to lower, front to back, left to right, and whether one area is carrying the whole photo while another is lagging.

03 · Conditioning

Conditioning, not diagnosis

Leanness, definition, and softness affect the read. Body fat is shown only as a rough visual estimate, not a body composition measurement and not medical truth.

04 · Readability

Photo quality limits

Lighting, pose, pump, camera angle, cropping, blur, clothing, and body fat can all change the result. Bad inputs get a bad read, or no read at all.

CALLOUT does not show percentile rankings — there is no audited population behind it to rank you against. Scores describe how this photo reads to the model, nothing more. Treat the output as directional feedback, then go lift.

What happens to the photo you upload.

You're sending us a body photo, so here is its exact path — the actual pipeline, not vague reassurance.

01 · On your device

Resized in your browser first

Before anything is uploaded, the photo is downscaled and converted to JPEG on your own device. The original file never leaves your phone or computer.

02 · In transit

Encrypted, sent for one job

The resized image travels over HTTPS to our server for a single purpose: this scan. It is not written to a database, a gallery, or a feed.

03 · The scan

Graded by an AI vision model

Anthropic's model reads the image and returns the grade, processing it under its own terms. CALLOUT does not store the photo and never uses it for training.

04 · After

Your result stays on your device

The result card and photo preview live only in your browser. Payments run entirely on Stripe — card details never touch CALLOUT.

CALLOUT uses an AI vision model to estimate visible muscle development from the photo you upload. It is not a medical tool, a DEXA scan, or a body-composition measurement.

C means the photo shows a visible training base, but also obvious work left. It does not mean you're impressive from every angle — that's B and above. It's an honest baseline, not an insult.

No. CALLOUT only sees the photo. It cannot detect substance use, training history, injuries, or health status.

A coach can understand context and help you plan. CALLOUT is faster and more direct: it gives a consistent AI read of the photo, with the limits of photo-based AI clearly stated.
