Questions

Plain answers.

Is the free scan really free?
Yes. One angle, fully graded, no card required. Just an email to reveal your result.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no questions.
How accurate is the grading?
It uses Claude AI vision to assess visible muscle development against competitive standards. It's honest, not flattering.
What happens to my photos?
Your photo is sent directly to our AI for grading and is not stored on our servers. We do not use your images for training data or share them with third parties. Once the analysis is complete, the image is gone.
Does it work for women?
Yes. The grading system assesses visible muscle development, conditioning, and proportion — the same criteria apply regardless of gender. The tiers are calibrated against the full training population, not a male-only standard.
What does a C grade actually mean?
C means you train. You have a visible base and you're consistent. It does not mean you're impressive to an experienced eye — that's B and above. Most people who go to the gym regularly land in C. It's an honest baseline, not an insult.
Is this for natural lifters only?
PHYSIQ grades visible development from photos — it cannot detect substances. The grading scale is calibrated against the full competitive population including enhanced athletes at the top end, so natural lifters at the same development level will score the same.
How is this different from just asking a PT or posting on Reddit?
A PT will encourage you. Reddit will argue. PHYSIQ gives you a consistent, calibrated score with no ego protection and no agenda — the same standard applied every time, to everyone.
