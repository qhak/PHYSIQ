What CALLOUT can and can't judge.

It grades what the photo gives it ŌĆö nothing more.

01 ┬Ę Development

Visible muscle development

CALLOUT looks for visible size, shape, separation, and how much trained tissue is actually readable in the image. If a muscle is hidden, it should not get a confident score.

02 ┬Ę Shape

Proportion and symmetry

It compares what is visible across the frame: upper to lower, front to back, left to right, and whether one area is carrying the whole photo while another is lagging.

03 ┬Ę Conditioning

Conditioning, not diagnosis

Leanness, definition, and softness affect the read. Body fat is shown only as a rough visual estimate, not a body composition measurement and not medical truth.

04 ┬Ę Readability

Photo quality limits

Lighting, pose, pump, camera angle, cropping, blur, clothing, and body fat can all change the result. Bad inputs get a bad read, or no read at all.

CALLOUT does not show percentile rankings ŌĆö there is no audited population behind it to rank you against. Scores describe how this photo reads to the model, nothing more. Treat the output as directional feedback, then go lift.
